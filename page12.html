Every moment 💖

With you… everything feels right

Main character energy. Always.

Every moment I spend with you…

becomes my favourite moment.

It doesn’t matter what we’re doing…

talking nonsense…

laughing for no reason…

or just sitting quietly…

everything feels better with you.

Every random moment…

somehow becomes special.

And I think that’s the best part about us 💖

Even silence feels comfortable with you.
