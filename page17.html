Mahi… 💌

I don’t know how to say this properly

As I’m writing this…

I’m actually emotional.

We’ve known each other for so many years…

and you’ve been a part of almost every phase of my life.

I’ve grown…

from being immature…

to someone who actually tries to understand things better…

and a big part of that is because of you.

Thank you…

for being there for me when I needed you the most.

When I was vulnerable…

when I was at my lowest…

you stayed.

You’ve been my unpaid therapist, my human diary…

my safe place.

You are my home…

away from home.

And no matter what happens…

no matter how many people come into our lives…

no matter how far we are…

you will always be my best friend.

The bestest of the best 💖

I love you to the moon and back…

I love you infinitely.

I will always choose you.

You are my home. No matter where I go, it’s always you.
