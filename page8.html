Why you matter 💖

This is something I don’t say enough

You were there for me…

when nobody else was.

When I was at my lowest…

when I didn’t even understand myself…

you stayed.

You listened to me talk about the same thing again and again…

and you never once made me feel like I was too much.

You didn’t judge me.

You didn’t just throw advice at me.

Instead…

you helped me understand myself.

You helped me grow.

And honestly…

you believed in me more than I believed in myself.

That’s what makes you different.

That’s what makes you irreplaceable 💖

You are my safe place.

My safe place. Always has been, always will be.
