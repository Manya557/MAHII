That phase 💔

8 November 2023… something I’ll never forget

We had a terrible fight.

It started with a misunderstanding…

but it turned into something much bigger.

People got involved… things got messy… (coz of Messi)

and suddenly…

we weren’t us anymore.

We stopped talking.

And for the first time…

I felt like I lost you.

That scared me more than anything.

I’m really… truly sorry for everything that went wrong.

Because no fight… no misunderstanding…

is ever worth losing you.

I never want to feel that again.
