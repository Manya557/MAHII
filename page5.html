The little things 💖

From school corridors to random road walks… we never changed.

You probably never noticed this…

You know something?

I used to pick up your pencils… whenever they fell from your pouch.

And instead of giving them back immediately…

I would keep them.

Just so I had a reason to talk to you the next day.

That’s how much I wanted to be around you.

It wasn’t about the pencil…

it was about you.

And honestly…

even today, nothing has changed 💖

Yeah… I was that desperate 😭
